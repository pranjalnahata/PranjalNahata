Now about myself

Fueled by boredom during pandemic lockdown, coupled with a strong drive to explore, I created my first Instagram Effect in April 2020. To my absolute surprise, it reached over 11M impressions. That is how I was born in this world of AR and there is no looking back now.

Over the past 3 years, I have gradually upskilled myself, worked for some lovely local and international brands, and have networked with a bunch of equally enthusiastic creators. Additionally, I am a co-organizer of XDG Mumbai where we hold monthly meetups to co-ideate on effects and give roots to budding developers.

Currently, I am a full-time freelancer, eager to connect with you to help you take your brand into a dreamy AR Journey, far beyond traditional limits.
